ABOUT US

Building with purpose and precision

We deliver sustainable, high-quality projects that strengthen communities and inspire progress.

AOS Project Management Limited is an indigenous service-based organisation registered over three years ago with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) under Registration No. 1263091. The company provides professional services within the real estate sector, maintaining a commitment to quality and reliability.

AOS Project Management Limited is also a civil and building construction company with over a decade of experience in delivering excellence. Our operations cover building construction, road construction, haulage services, dredging, and shore protection, consistently meeting the highest industry standards.

Over the years, we have successfully executed more than 300 diverse construction projects, with a major focus on real estate development. Our track record reflects our dedication to precision, durability, and client satisfaction across every completed project.

Our team comprises highly experienced, innovative, and dynamic professionals with proven expertise in both the real estate and financial industries, working together to drive growth, trust, and lasting value in every engagement.
